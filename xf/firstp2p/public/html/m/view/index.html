" ABOUT US "
Who are we?
NCF (www.ncfwx.com) is established in July 2013.NCF is based on investment and financing services and wealth management, set the most advanced technology, operation management and marketing promotion, in order to provide multiple services for financial institutions and entities industry. NCF is committed to building a world-leading open fin-tech ecosystem.
What are we doing?
As an open fintech ecosystem, the NCF platform exports fintech technology, provides financial institutions and non-financial institutions with online user access, gives users one-stop integrated financial information service and has adopted the sharing model of Internet finance services.
Our service objects
NCF is committed to provide multiple services for Small and medium enterprises and individuals.
Our business mode
NCF satisfies the diversified investment needs of ecosystem users through multi-scenario investment experience, thus forming a closed-loop ecosystem in the field of Internet finance. The ecosystem provides a variety of online technology functions, such as investor diversion, dedicated financial services zone, information transmission and so on, which not only makes every link of financial transactions safe and fast, but also provides convenient technical support for financing parties to issue financing needs.
